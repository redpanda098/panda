Cricket

is a bat-and-ball game played between two teams of eleven players on a field at the center of which is a 
22-yard pitch
with a wicket at each end, each comprising two bails balanced on three stumps. The batting side scores runs by striking the ball bowled at the wicket with the bat, and attempting to run to the opposite wicket, while thebowling and fielding side tries to prevent this and
dismiss
each player (so they are out).
